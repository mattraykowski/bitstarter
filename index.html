At Premin we believe that preschool is not the same as day care. We believe you'd rather be playing with and teaching children, and not hassling with paperwork, forms and keeping track of notices.

Premin is designed from the beginning with the unique challenges, regulations and workflows required of preschools in mind. It eases the administrative tasks involved with educating preschoolers and provides a unique and exciting way for teachers and parents to collaborate.

Premin privides a portal for parents and teachers to connect. It helps teachers and program administrators to reduce their workload and aims to limit or eliminate unnecessary paperwork. Premin automates your important administrative tasks and helps schools to focus on their real goals: educating preschoolers.

FAQ

What is Premin?
Premin is a cloud-based preschool management application which provides electronic enrollment, mandatory form management (such as vaccinations, special needs and allergies), and a centralized platform for student/family communication and collaboration. Premin provides teachers and program administrators an easy way of managing the vast amount of paperwork and information that is required to run a preschool. Everything from school specific forms (saved as PDF attachments) to regulatory documentation parents may be required to submit.
Who is Premin for?
Premin is designed for small-to-medium preschools with a focus on education rather than child care. There are a lot of child care applications which do well at the needs of daycare but do not cater towards educational facilities. Program Administrators and Lead Teachers are usually most interested in solutions like Premin.
How does my school benefit?
Premin streamlines your enrollment process through an intuitive wizard that can be linked to easily from your website. Returning families and students can fast forward through the process, saving time. A simplified data entry process means that parents who prefer pen-and-paper don't add a substantial burden on teachers and administrators. Having everything electronic and in the cloud means that no leaky roof, storm or fire will destroy years or decades worth of stored documents.
How do our families benefit?
In addition to the streamline enrollment process Premin provides a number of ways for families to collaborate with your school and your teachers. Parents can easily contact their child's teacher through their parent portal, keeping teacher information private and having one consistent place for communication. Administrators and and Lead Teachers can quickly and easily send an individual family, an the families of an entire class or all currently enrolled families notices and alerts. Teachers can post class-specific information to their online class rooms for parents to download, such as worksheets or newsletters. It also provides a central place for families to find and read their child's information such as student portfolios and standardized testing results.
How do our teachers benefit?
Teachers have a one-stop-shop for electronic communication with their families. From their Teacher Portal they are be able to see at a glance everything about their classes. They're able to broadcast messages to their families or target messages to specific families. They can send alertes in the same manner - where as alerts require some action by the parents or guardians, such as acknowledgement or logging in to upload a mandatory form. Teachers have an Online Class Room to represent their class where they can post information, newsletters and picture galleries for families to view.
Having all of their class information online means that classes with multiple active teachers can do their work from home or at the school and the other teachers will immediately see their changes. No more double work or rework - all forms and notices are performed from the same interface. No embarassing double emails from different teachers with conflicting information.
